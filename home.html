Our Philosophy

Refined Living in the
Heart of Solwezi

Nestville Apartments was designed for those who refuse to compromise on comfort, even when far from home. Every unit is thoughtfully furnished, blending warm African aesthetics with modern convenience — a sanctuary for business travellers, families, and extended stays.

Situated on Kabitaka Road, we are minutes from Solwezi's central business district, surrounded by lush greenery yet fully connected to the city.
